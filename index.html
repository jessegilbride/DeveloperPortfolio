About Me

I graduated with a BS in Computer Engineering from Wentworth IT (Boston, MA) and while I excelled and enjoyed my education greatly, the job market after the economic crash of 2008 left me seeking a career that allowed me to be more creative and hopefully recession-proof. So I embarked on becoming a Front End Web Developer by learning as much as I could through books, web tutorials, and peeking at what other great devs have done (via the browser's code inspector, of course).

After five years of working in the field, though, I found that it was getting very difficult to pick up certain skills that are crucial for today's web developers to be marketable and successful, which led me Thinkful. I'm very excited to discover what amazing things I will build as I go through the coursework. As time progresses, I will add those works (or links to them) on this site for all to see.
